What I Do

3D Modelling

In my sophomore year at STEM School, I had the distinct honor of being selected as one of Eight students to join the exclusive Fab Lab Club . Over the course of three years, I actively participated in the Fab Lab, engaging in various projects that allowed me to master state-of-the-art tools such as laser cutters, 3D printers, and Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software, including the proficient use of SolidWorks for intricate designs. This unique experience not only deepened my technical expertise but also provided valuable insights into the realm of digital fabrication. Additionally, I actively contributed to numerous workshops, further enhancing my understanding of diverse tools and their applications in innovative projects.

App Development

I am actively involved in the development of a unique personal application that serves as a comprehensive platform to present my diverse portfolio and provides an in-depth exploration of various aspects of my background and experiences. This app aims to offer a more detailed and interactive understanding of my skills, projects, and accomplishments, providing a dynamic and engaging showcase of my professional journey.

Graphic Design

I play a pivotal role in the Design board for STEM Model Arab League, contributing significantly to the creation of eye-catching posts and the design of the team's seasonal shirt. My involvement extends beyond this, as I actively participate in crafting the overall visual aesthetic for the STEM October Sports Club page, where I dedicate my skills to produce a variety of captivating designs. This collaborative effort involves a diverse range of design projects, showcasing my commitment to enhancing the visual appeal and branding of these initiatives.

Leadership

Student Union

    As a leader and student union representative, I have demonstrated strong organizational skills and a proactive approach in shaping meaningful initiatives. I have successfully orchestrated scientific competitions for grade 10 and 11 students, including prestigious events like TOFAS, Egyptian Qundus, and the ISEF competition. These initiatives have fostered intellectual growth and provided students with opportunities to excel. Through strategic planning and collaboration, I have contributed to the academic enrichment and holistic development of our student body, positively impacting the school's educational landscape.
    During the academic year, we organized two enriching field trips to the renowned Dokki Research Center to enhance students' educational experience. These trips provided students with invaluable support and guidance, allowing them to interact directly with resident professors, gain profound insights, and foster collaborative learning environments. These trips served as a catalyst for academic growth and experiential learning, solidifying our commitment to providing holistic educational opportunities for our students. The primary objective was to contribute to the success of their capstone projects.
    Demonstrating proactive leadership and a commitment to fostering a vibrant school community, we took the commendable initiative of orchestrating not just one, but two unforgettable fundays that left an indelible mark on the collective memory of our institution. A staggering total of approximately 450 students were joyously brought together during these meticulously planned events, creating an atmosphere of camaraderie, excitement, and shared experiences.
    I have organized two trips to El Fayoum and Port Said, aiming to provide enriching experiences for students. The trips, which were planned and executed with the participation of around 100 students, were both educational and recreational. The goal was to create lasting memories and foster camaraderie among students. The trips included diverse activities and cultural explorations, allowing students to fully immerse themselves in the rich heritage and unique landscapes of these locations. The success of these ventures demonstrates our commitment to holistic student development and creating memorable experiences beyond traditional classroom settings.
    Representing our school at various events, including the Water World Day, where distinguished guests like Mr. Christian Burger, the European Union's representative in Egypt, and Hany Swelim, the Minister of Irrigation in Egypt, were present. These events provided opportunities to interact with key figures and prominent individuals in Egypt. Furthermore, participating in workshops focused on recycling and environmental sustainability. These experiences led to my selection as one of the speakers to represent our school and offer innovative solutions to reduce plastic usage within our educational institution
